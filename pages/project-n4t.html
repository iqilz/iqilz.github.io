N4T

Client Project

Lead Designer

2025

This is a short animation sequence made for artist N4T. It was not used in the end but I created 2 short animated sequences that followed his y2k style for the music video. My role involved creating 3D assets with high-gloss chrome finishes and integrated 'star' motifs to achieve a nostalgic yet polished aesthetic. A primary technical challenge was compositing these heavy 3D extrusions into live-action environments to ensure they felt grounded in the scene's lighting and depth. Though these sequences were ultimately not included in the final cut, they serve as an exploration of bold typography and artist-centric visual world-building.
